3. Connect to Spotify

Download

Installation instructions (Mac OS):

  1. Download the zip file
  2. If it doesn't exist, create a Spotify folder in your home folder.
  3. Extract the zip file into the folder
  4. Get a free Spotify Developer Account
  5. Enter spotify:app:fredbox into Spotify's search box.
  6. When you're ready to party, enter the name of your playlist

4. Party!

Here's how you party the hardest.

Share your Fredbox with your party guests. Ask them to submit songs and upvote them. Then, as soon as your playlist is ready to roll and the party is about to start, open the Spotify app.

When you enter your playlist's name into Spotify, it will take the song at the top of the list and play it. Whenever a song is over, it will take whatever's at the top at the moment.
